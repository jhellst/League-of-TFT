Notes

The ultimate goal of TFT is to be the last player standing in an 8-player free-for-all match. You spend the game accumulating resources like gold and items and making decisions to build a team of units with different strengths, weaknesses, and abilities.

A player in a game of Teamfight Tactics is referred to as a "Summoner".

All statistics provided are calculated over the selected range of matches entered under "Match Count".

Description of Provided Statistics

  • Average Damage: The average amount of damage dealt by the summoner per game
  • Average Placement: The summoner's average placement per game (possible placements of 1st through 8th place).
  • Average Players Eliminated: The average number of eliminations a summoner has per game (possible eliminations between 0 and 7).
  • Average Final Level: The average level the summoner reached per game (possible levels between 1 and 9).
